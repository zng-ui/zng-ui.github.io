pub fn slerp(self, to: Self, factor: Factor) -> Self

Spherical linear interpolation.

Always uses the shortest path from self to to.

The lerp linear interpolation always covers the numeric range between angles, so a transition from 358º to 1º iterates over almost a full counterclockwise turn to reach the final value, slerp simply goes from 358º to 361º modulo normalized.
